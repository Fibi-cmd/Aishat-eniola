🐟 Professional Fish Egg Hatching

1. Water Preparation

Maintain pH 6.5-7.5 • Temperature 28°C • Use methylene blue (1mg/L)

2. Egg Selection

Choose translucent eggs • Remove white opaque eggs • Density: 2-3 eggs/cm²

3. Incubation Protocol

Water flow 0.5L/min • 16h light/8h dark cycle • Oxygen >6mg/L

🐔 Premium Chicken Incubation

1. Egg Preparation

Store <48hrs • 13-18°C • 75% humidity • Never wash eggs

2. Incubation Setup

Days 1-18: 37.5°C • 60% humidity • Turn 3x daily

Days 19-21: 37.2°C • 70% humidity • Stop turning

3. Hatching Protocol

Increase O₂ levels • Maintain darkness • First pipping at 490hrs

×

🐔 Ƙyanƙyashe Ƙwai na Kaza

1. Shirya Ƙwai

Ajiye <48hrs • 13-18°C • Danshi 75% • Kada a wanke ƙwai

2. Tsarin Ƙyanƙyashe

Kwanaki 1-18: 37.5°C • Danshi 60% • Juya sau 3 a rana

Kwanaki 19-21: 37.2°C • Danshi 70% • Daina juyawa

3. Tsarin Hatching

Ƙara yawan O₂ • Ci gaba da duhu • Farkon fashewa a cikin sa'o'i 490

⚙️ Advanced Incubator Build

Materials Needed:

- STM32 microcontroller
- 100W PTC heater
- Ultrasonic humidifier
- 12V DC motor for turning
- DHT22 sensor

Assembly Guide:

1. Insulate 60L container with 50mm foam
2. Install heater+fan circulation system
3. Program PID temperature control
4. Set automatic turning mechanism
